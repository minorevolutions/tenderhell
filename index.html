Tender Hell presents the culminating thesis work of the 2026 Yale Graphic Design MFA class. The exhibition aims to showcase the diverse range of individual practices within the cohort, while acknowledging the often fraught relationship design has to labor, power, service, and circulation.

In addition to the work presented in the gallery, each participant will also produce materials to be distributed during the show.
